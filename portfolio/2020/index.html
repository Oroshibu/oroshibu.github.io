OCTOBER 2020

GUNWYRM - Team Bugulon

Game Maker Studio 2

GUNWYRM is an action-arcade game based around endlessly upgrading and controlling mechanical serpents. The Gunwyrm is tasked with destroying invaders, gathering parts to make its next iteration even more powerful. When the timer runs out, the next Gunwyrm activates, and the cycle continues!

Made in 72 hours for the Ludum Dare 47 Game Jam.
➜ Ranked 53rd out of 2406 entries.
➜ Newgrounds Daily Feature Third Place October 8 2020.

APRIL 2020

Lymphosite - Team Bugulon

Game Maker Studio 2

The immune crystal is under attack! Plentiful germs, parasites and viruses come to plague the once healthy immune crystal... Just when all hope seems lost, the vigilant Pill Ship comes to save the day, no disease can combat this scrapper's arsenal.
Collect the blood your adversaries drop in order to relieve the crystal, obtain upgrades from the shop and gun germs down with many guns to your disposal!

Made in 72 hours for the Ludum Dare 46 Game Jam.
➜ Ranked 114th out of 3576 entries.

MARCH 2020

Hivemind - Team Bugulon

Game Maker Studio 2

Hivemind is an action-arcade game with a heavy focus of power in numbers. Under the threat of extermination, the great Hive King takes matters into his own hands to stop those threatening his presence. Recklessly using his loyal bee subjects, he can take back what once belonged to him before the wild life and Hazmats eliminate him once and for all. None shall usurp him of his power!

Made in 48 hours for the Stay Safe! Game Jam.
➜ Ranked 4th in popularity out of 255 entries.
